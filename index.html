About Me

I am Lucas Wolman, based in London, UK. I hold a First-Class BA in Manufacturing Engineering from Trinity Hall, Cambridge, and will be pursuing an MSc in Transport with Data Science at Imperial College London.

Operating at the intersection of technology startups and sustainable infrastructure, I bridge the gap between analytical rigour and operational execution. My recent projects include driving go-to-market operations for Founders Match and managing complex construction logistics for the Wild Mile floating eco-park with Urban Rivers.

Professional Goals: To leverage machine learning, statistical modelling, and operational strategy to optimise large-scale systems, particularly in sustainable transport.

Interests: Vice-Captain and Coxswain for the Trinity Hall Boat Club, Grade 8 singer, and Jazz Saxophonist.
